How does it work

To meet today’s tsunami of highly disruptive and ever growing government legislation, KS Tax manages your eGovernment submissions end to end.

1

Collect & Gather

  • Gather data from existing system
  • Carry out extensive data validation
  • Prepare data for storage
2

Prepare, Submit & Validate

  • Collect & Clean
  • Validate prior to submission
  • One touch submission
3

Clarify & Confirm

  • Real time two way communication
  • Full error and status management
  • Integrated global analytics
